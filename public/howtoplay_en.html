How to Play

Survival Game "bivouac" - Game Guide

Game Objective

Main Goal

This is a multiplayer survival game where players collect vaccines from the field and eliminate zombies to survive.

  • Cooperate with other players to collect vaccines
  • Vaccine count is shared among all players
  • Game is cleared when a certain number of vaccines are collected
  • Manage health, hunger, and thirst while surviving

About Vaccines

Vaccine Mechanism

Vaccines are scattered throughout the world, locked inside keyboxes.

  • Vaccine locations are displayed in the player's view, so find the vaccines and eliminate the zombies
  • To unlock a vaccine keybox, you need to decrypt the code near the keybox for a certain period of time
  • After a certain time passes, the keybox will be unlocked and you can obtain the vaccine
  • The number of vaccines obtained is shared across the world and affects the world's difficulty level

Controls

PC Version

WASD - Move
Mouse - Look around & Shoot
E - Use/Pick up items
B - Open/Close backpack
R - Warp
Space - Jump

Mobile Version

Left Joystick - Move
Tap Screen - Shoot
Backpack Button - Item management
Warp Button - Teleport
Camera Button - Change view

Status Management

Health

Indicates overall physical condition. Decreases during combat and environmental damage. Game over when it reaches 0.

Hunger

Decreases over time. Can be restored by eating food.

Thirst

Decreases over time. Can be restored by drinking water.

Stamina

Decreases gradually when running and recovers over time. Cannot run when stamina reaches 0.

Important Notes

Key Points

  • Cooperation with other players is essential
  • Always monitor your status and manage it appropriately
  • Items are limited, so use them efficiently
  • Zombies are powerful, avoid reckless combat
  • Vaccine collection is the top priority
